### Priority 1 — Blocking (must fix for GO)

1. **Execution pipeline bottleneck (RT-J01, RT-N01):** The execution engine can't keep up with the detection rate, causing `execution-requests` to hit MAXLEN and silently trim messages. Two fixes needed:

   - **Execution throughput:** Consider increasing `MAX_CONCURRENT_EXECUTIONS` or optimizing the execution pipeline. Current simulated execution at ~10/s vs detection at ~417 opps/s.

2. **Fly.io health check port (SA-4Q1):** `infrastructure/fly/execution-engine.toml` checks use port 8080 but service listens on 3005. Update `[checks]` sections to use `port = 3005`.
